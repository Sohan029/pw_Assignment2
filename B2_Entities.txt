 HTML Entities :

 HTML entities are special characters that cannot be easily typed on a keyboard 
 or are reserved for use in HTML code. 

 Here are five commonly used HTML entities:

 1. <	less than	            &lt;	&#60;	
 2. >	greater than	        &gt;	&#62;	
 3. &	ampersand	            &amp;	&#38;	
 4. "	double quotation mark	&quot;	&#34;	
 5. '	single quotation mark 	&apos;	&#39;
